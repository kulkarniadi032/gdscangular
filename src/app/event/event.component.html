30 days of Google cloud

30 days of google cloud program provides you an opportunity to kickstart your career in cloud and get hands-on practice on google cloud platform- the tool that powers apps like Google Search, Gmail and Youtube.

Android Study Jams

Android study jams are community are organized study groups for people to learn how to build androidd apps using the kotlin programming language, using the curriculum provided by google.
Solution Challenge
The mission of the 2022 Solution Challenge is to solve for one or more of the United Nations 17 Sustainable Development Goals using Google technology. The Solution Challenge is open to members of Google Developer Student Clubs.
