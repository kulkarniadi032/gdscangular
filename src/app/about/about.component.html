About Our Institution

“DIEMS, established in the academic year 2009-10, is one of the renowned Engineering and Management institute of the Aurangabad region. It is affiliated to the Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere and functions under All India Council of Technical Education (A.I.C.T.E.), Government of India.” Since 2009, DIEMS has been guiding students with best up-to-date facilities, experienced faculties and passionate management committees.
