Deogiri Institute Of Engineering And Management Studies

Deogiri Institute of Engineering and Management Studies is established at Aurangabad during the academic year 2009-10, in fulfillment with the policies of the Government of India to start Engineering colleges in emerging technologies.

Deogiri Institute of Engineering and Management studies has 100+ highly experienced, dedicated and motivating Faculty Members. They aim at providing the best and industrial ready graduates. Students learn both therotical and practical ways to go around academics.

DIEMS has shown consistent growth since the beginning. With it’s ever-growing placements and top results in University. Today, DIEMS is home to more than 1000 students, enrolled in various undergraduates, post-graduates and masters programs.

Welcome To Our Campus

Deogiri Institute of Engineering and Management Studies is an undergraduate and postgraduate, coeducational college along with Research center in many disciplines, located in Aurangabad, Maharashtra. It was established in the year 1960 by Marathwada Shikshan Prasarak Mandal.
The college is affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University. it has various clubs for college students in which they can master their skills by participating.
